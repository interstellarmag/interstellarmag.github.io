Shivali H. Patra

Graphic Designer & Staff Writer

Shivali is a freshman based in the Bay Area with a strong passion for both the arts and STEM. She’s actively involved in her school’s arts program, contributing as a writer for the yearbook and serving on the editorial board of the literary arts magazine, Soundings. Alongside her role at Interstellar, where she writes articles focused on biology and medicine, Shivali enjoys crafting free-verse poetry and runs her website, Faces of Silicon Valley, where she explores a wide range of different topics.
As one of Interstellar’s PR Managers and Staff Writers, Shivali designed many of its early Instagram posts and loves creating engaging and mini infographics. Outside of the arts, she loves spending time with her family, reading, and listening to music while studying.
She is incredibly excited to be part of Interstellar’s journey and is proud of how much it has grown. Shivali looks forward to sharing the magazine with readers and helping others discover the beauty and intricacies of scientific thinking.
