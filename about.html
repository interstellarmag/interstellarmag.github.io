About Us

Our origin story

Our original staff attended UC Santa Cruz COSMOS in 2025, where we were members of the newsletter team.

Having found passion in explaining cool, practically applicable science, we released our first issue in October 2025.

This issue featured articles from people who would go on to become our fantastic staff writers. Being the backbone of the whole magazine, we value their contributions greatly!

If you want this recognition too, submit an article!

What we're doing now

Since then, highschool students from all around the world have joined our team, creating a vibrant and collaborative community dedicated to bringing you articles about STEM topics you would actually enjoy.

Releasing articles that we're proud of has been immensely fulfilling, and we are eternally grateful for the support from readers like you!

We have also grown our social media presence to make learning about STEM topics more accessible even to those who don't have time to read a full article.

You can find our content on @interstellar_mag on Instagram, though we suggest you follow our new account, @interstellar.stem, since we have a surprise for the future!

If our mission interests you, you can apply to be a writer or artist via this form! We welcome all teens / highschool students.
